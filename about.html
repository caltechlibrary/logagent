About this software

logagent 0.0.4

Working proof of concept fo LogAgent and LogAnalyst.

Authors

  • R. S. Doiel, https://orcid.org/0000-0003-0900-6903

LogAgent was designed detecting bot based on log entries searching for specific sub strings. LogAnalyst was written to aggregate the structure in our logs to provide simple count relations between log fields. The are intended to work with the NginX access logs we generate from our RDM instances. The tooling may work with other types of logs.
